The Solution

60 seconds at the booth.
Everything else is automatic.

01
📸
Capture your way
Open the MeetSend app in your browser and snap the badge or business card — no download, no login. Prefer WhatsApp? Use your dedicated MeetSend number instead. Works however your team works, on any device.
5 seconds
02
🎤
Record a voice note
Hold record and speak freely for 30 seconds while walking to the next conversation. Pain points, buying signals, what you promised — raw is fine. No signal? MeetSend queues it and sends when you're back online.
30 seconds
03
✉️
Follow-up lands in your inbox
MeetSend extracts the contact, enriches the lead, transcribes your voice note, and generates a personalised follow-up email — delivered directly to the rep who captured the lead. Each person on your team gets their own emails. Syncs automatically to your CRM — Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive.
Under 5 mins
04
📊
View everything in one place
Every lead, every follow-up, every event — visible in your MeetSend dashboard. See who followed up, who hasn't, lead scores, and pipeline value generated. Full team visibility. Zero spreadsheets.
Real-time
Team Intelligence

The right lead.
The right rep. Automatically.

For teams, capturing the lead is only half the job. MeetSend's intelligence layer reads every lead the moment it comes in — seniority, company size, industry, buying signals — and routes it to the rep best suited to close it. No manager triage. No Monday morning spreadsheet. No high-intent leads going cold over the weekend.

🎯
Intent-based escalation
When MeetSend detects high-intent signals — a decision maker, an active budget, a short buying timeline — it flags the lead as priority and routes it directly to a senior AE or the account owner, bypassing the standard queue.
Hot leads never wait
🏢
Industry-based assignment
Configure your team by vertical — fintech, healthcare, manufacturing, SaaS. MeetSend reads the prospect's industry from enrichment data and assigns them to the rep who knows that space, so the follow-up lands with genuine context.
Specialist rep, every time
👤
Seniority routing
A C-suite lead captured by a junior rep doesn't sit in their inbox. MeetSend detects title seniority and escalates — alerting a senior AE or sales director in real time, with the full lead context already attached.
C-suite gets senior attention
🗺️
Territory management
Running a team across regions? Map your territories once. MeetSend routes every lead to the rep who owns that geography — automatically, from the moment the badge is scanned.
Clean territory, no overlap
Enterprise
Custom routing logic for your team structure
Build rules around any combination of industry, company size, seniority, geography, or buying signals. Plug directly into your CRM ownership rules. Works with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive out of the box.
Features

Everything your sales team
actually needs.

🤖
Individualised Follow-Ups
Powered by AI, every email sounds like you wrote it in 3 minutes after a great conversation — because it references the real conversation you had.
  • Personalised to their role, pain, and timeline
  • One clear call to action per email
  • Human tone, zero buzzwords
🔍
Automatic Lead Enrichment
A name and company becomes a full profile — company size, funding stage, tech stack, recent news — automatically.
  • LinkedIn profile data
  • Company size and industry
  • Recent company news and signals
📸
Badge & Card Scanning
Photograph any badge or business card. MeetSend reads it instantly — name, title, company — even from angled or imperfect photos.
  • Works at any event, no organiser API needed
  • Group photos — scan 3 badges at once
  • Business cards, printed badges, digital profiles
🎯
Lead Scoring
Every lead gets scored automatically — Hot, Warm, or Cool — based on buying signals in your voice note. Know who to call first.
  • Based on timeline, budget, and intent signals
  • Reasoning explained, not just a score
  • Suggested follow-up timing per lead
👥
Group Conversations — One Voice Note, Three Personalised Emails
Met a decision maker, a champion, and a technical evaluator from the same company? MeetSend generates three separate emails, each angled to that person's role and priorities. The decision maker gets the ROI email. The champion gets the implementation email. The CTO gets the technical specs email.
  • Role-based email angles generated automatically
  • One voice note covers the entire group
  • Each contact enriched and scored individually
